About Saudi Abaya style

Abaya designs are the most prominent features of harmony in which the culture, customs and traditions of the peoples of the Arab Gulf converge and are embodied in the fashion industry that is popular in the Gulf markets and appears in them, in addition to drawing the attention of Saudi and Gulf society to the importance of preserving the cultural identity in traditional dress and introducing it as a heritage creativity that reflects The artistic and aesthetic sense that characterizes women in the regions of Saudi Arabia.” Fashion designer Amina asserts, “The traditional dress, like the abaya, is not just a dress, but rather an artistic painting created by the hands of Saudi designers, in which they deposit their memories, identity, and belonging.”
